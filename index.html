About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at IIIT Vadodara–International Campus Diu. My research lies in quantum dynamics and quantum many-body theory, with a particular focus on collision processes involving electrons, positrons, and photons interacting with atoms, molecules, and their ions.

My work involves theoretical studies of atomic, molecular, and ionic systems, with applications to astrophysical plasmas, non-LTE environments, and fundamental processes in plasma physics. In particular, I am interested in atomic processes relevant to kilonovae and other transient astrophysical phenomena.

Research Interests

  • Dielectronic recombination in low charged ions
  • Atomic data for astrophysical plasmas
  • Electron and positron scattering
  • Quantum many-body theory
