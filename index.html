About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at IIIT Vadodara-International Campus Diu. My research focuses on quantum dynamics, quantum many-body theory, and collision processes involving electrons, positrons, and photons interacting with atoms, molecules, and ions. My work is particularly relevant to astrophysical plasmas and kilonovae.

Research Interests

  • Dielectronic recombination in low charged ions
  • Atomic data for astrophysical plasmas
  • Electron and positron scattering
  • Quantum many-body theory

Selected Publications

  • Dielectronic recombination studies of ions relevant to Kilonovae (A&A, 2025)
  • Dielectronic recombination studies on Fe2+ (Phys. Rev. A, 2025)
  • Metastable states of Si− (Phys. Rev. A, 2021)
  • Electron scattering for anthracene and pyrene (J. Phys. Chem. A, 2020)
